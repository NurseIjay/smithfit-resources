3-Step Activation Checklist

Complete these within your first 7 days. This sets the foundation for everything.

1

Book Your 60-Minute Gameplan Call

Your priority Calendly link is in your welcome email. This is a full 60 minutes, one-on-one with me. We will map out your entire 6-month plan together.

2

Upload Bloodwork to BioSync Portal

Upload your most recent labs. We will turn raw numbers into a personalized metabolic report you can actually use.

What to Upload

CBC, CMP (comprehensive metabolic panel), thyroid panel (TSH, Free T3, Free T4), and hormone panel if available. If you do not have recent labs, we will discuss next steps on your gameplan call.

3

Set Up MealLens on Your Phone

MealLens turns your phone camera into a nutrition tool. Four simple steps to get started.

  • Snap a photo of any meal
  • Add a quick description (optional but improves accuracy)
  • Get your instant macro and nutrient breakdown
  • Use it daily to build awareness (not perfection)

Gameplan Call Prep

What to Bring

  • A rough idea of what you have eaten the last 30 days (no tracking needed, just patterns)
  • Your sleep pattern: when you go to bed, when you wake up, quality
  • Your biggest struggles right now (energy, cravings, motivation, schedule)
  • Your goal for 6 months from now (be specific if you can)

What to Expect

This is a full 60 minutes. We will review your labs (if uploaded), talk through your shift schedule, identify your stress patterns, and build a personalized blueprint. You will leave the call with a clear plan, not a generic template.

BioSync Portal

What You Upload

  • CBC (Complete Blood Count)
  • CMP (Comprehensive Metabolic Panel)
  • Thyroid: TSH, Free T3, Free T4
  • Hormones (if available): estradiol, progesterone, testosterone

What You Get Back

A personalized metabolic report that translates your labs into actionable insights. Not just "your TSH is normal." Instead: what your numbers mean for your energy, your fat loss, and your shift recovery. This report feeds directly into your gameplan call.
