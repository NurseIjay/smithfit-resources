The 2-Minute Med Room Reset

When the shift is spiraling, your nervous system needs a signal that you are safe. Box breathing delivers that signal in under 90 seconds.

1 Inhale 4 counts
2 Hold 4 counts
3 Exhale 4 counts
4 Hold 4 counts

Repeat x 4 rounds. That is it. You just told your nervous system you are safe.

What To Skip (Even If You're Starving)

These feel like fuel but sabotage your next four hours.

  • Vending Machine Candy Spikes blood sugar fast, drops you harder
  • Sugary Coffee Drinks Sugar crash hits when you need to be sharpest
  • Energy Drinks After 2pm Wrecks your sleep recovery window
  • Fried Cafeteria Food Heavy fat pulls blood flow from your brain

The 30-Second Reset (Anywhere)

Four steps. No equipment. No one even has to notice.

  • 1
    Shoulders Down Drop them away from your ears
  • 2
    Three Deep Breaths Slow, full, intentional
  • 3
    Hand on Heart Physical touch activates the vagus nerve
  • 4
    Say: "I am safe. I am here. I am okay." Done. Back to the floor.
