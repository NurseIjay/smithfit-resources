This is your direct line to your coach. Here is how to get the most out of it.

  • Ask clear, specific questions. "My energy crashed after nights this week, what should I adjust?" works better than "I feel bad."

Your 12-Month Roadmap

Months 1 through 3

Phase 1: Foundation

Nervous system regulation, shift-proof nutrition, and movement basics. You are building the operating system that everything else runs on. By Month 3, the Morning Cocktail, plate formula, and 15-minute workouts feel automatic.

Months 4 through 6

Phase 2: Breakthrough

You have the foundation. Now we push. Progressive workouts, nuanced nutrition timing, and deeper stress management. This is where the visible changes accelerate. Clothes fitting differently. Energy that lasts the whole shift.

Life throws curveballs. Schedule changes, holidays, sick weeks, overtime. We build resilience into your system so that a bad week does not erase a good month. You learn to pivot without spiraling.
