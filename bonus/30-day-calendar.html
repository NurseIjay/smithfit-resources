Your First 30 Days

The Shift-Proof Calendar. One focus per week. No overwhelm.

1

Stabilize

Days 1 through 7

This Week's Focus

Morning Cocktail + Box Breathing. That is it. Two habits, no more.

  • Day 1 Download the SmithFit app and set up your profile
  • Day 1 Make your first Morning Metabolic Cocktail
  • Day 2 Join the community and introduce yourself
  • Day 3 Complete your first check-in (energy, sleep, cravings)
  • Days 1-7 Log one meal per day in the app
  • This Week Attend your first group coaching call

What to Expect

The first few days are about your nervous system, not your waistline. You may notice calmer mornings, fewer afternoon crashes, and slightly deeper sleep. 87% of members feel a noticeable shift by Day 3. If you feel nothing yet, that is completely normal. Your body is recalibrating.

2

Shift-Proof Your Plate

Days 8 through 14

This Week's Focus

The 3x3 Plate Formula: Protein + Fiber + Fat at every meal.

  • Day 8 Learn the 3x3 Plate Formula in the Stabilize hub
  • Days 8-12 Apply the formula to at least 5 meals this week
  • This Week Use the Chaos Day Rescue plan at least once
  • This Week Attend one group coaching call
  • Day 14 Check in: how are your energy and cravings compared to Day 1?

What to Expect

You will likely notice that meals built with protein, fiber, and fat keep you fuller longer, even on 12-hour shifts. Cravings may soften. You are not dieting. You are building a plate that works with your stress physiology instead of against it.

3

Add Movement

Days 15 through 21

This Week's Focus

Three 15-minute cortisol-conscious workouts. We are not chasing sweat. We are sending a signal.

  • Day 15 Complete your first 15-minute workout (choose your level)
  • Days 15-21 Complete 3 workouts total this week
  • This Week Submit your weekly check-in
  • This Week Post once in the community (a win, a question, or just say hi)

What to Expect

These workouts are short by design. When cortisol is already elevated from shift work, long intense sessions can backfire. Fifteen minutes tells your body "we are safe, we are strong" without spiking stress hormones. You may notice improved mood and energy within hours of each session.

4

The Full Loop

Days 22 through 30

This Week's Focus

Run all three pillars together. Stabilize + Fuel + Move on autopilot.

  • Daily Morning Cocktail + Box Breathing (by now this should feel automatic)
  • Daily 3x3 Plate at most meals (progress, not perfection)
  • 3x/week 15-minute cortisol-conscious workouts
  • Day 28 Submit your weekly check-in
  • Day 30 Capture your transformation: take a photo, write down how you feel

What to Expect

This is where it clicks. The habits start to feel like yours, not a program you are following. Many members report that by Day 30, they are sleeping better, craving less sugar, fitting into clothes differently, and most importantly, feeling like themselves again.
