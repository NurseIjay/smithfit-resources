Week 2 Resource Card

Batch Cook Sunday

Three recipes. One afternoon. A whole week handled.

Recipe 1

Honey Garlic Chicken Thighs

Chicken thighs + soy sauce + honey + garlic + rice vinegar. 400 F for 25 min.

34g protein 5 servings 25 min

"Season, toss in a pan, walk away. Come back to 5 days of protein."

Recipe 2

Turkey Taco Meat + Rice

Ground turkey + taco seasoning + black beans + diced tomatoes. Stovetop 15 min. Serve over rice or in wraps.

28g protein 6 servings 15 min

"This is the meal that survives shift changes, picky kids, and midnight hunger. Versatile. Reliable."

Recipe 3

Overnight Protein Oat Jars

Oats + protein powder + milk + chia seeds + cinnamon. Stir in a mason jar. Fridge overnight. Grab and go.

22g protein 5 jars 10 min

"Prep all 5 in under 10 minutes. Eat cold on the drive to work."

The Game Plan

Your Sunday in 90 Minutes

  • 0:00 Season chicken, put in oven
  • 0:05 Start turkey taco meat on stovetop
  • 0:10 While both cook, assemble 5 oat jars
  • 0:20 Turkey done. Portion into containers.
  • 0:25 Chicken done. Let cool 10 min, then portion.
  • 0:35 Label containers: Mon / Tue / Wed / Thu / Fri

Done. You just bought yourself a week of not thinking about food.
